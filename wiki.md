## Getting started

### Guide Book

The **Guide Book** (`better_on_bedrock:guide_book`) opens the in-game guide.

| Action | Result |
| --- | --- |
| Use | Opens the guide menu. |
| Crouch + use | Opens credits. |
| Place the book entity | Lets you page through the in-world model. |

Ways to obtain it:

- New survival players receive it during onboarding.
- Craft with Dirt.
- Use `/guidebook`, `/better_on_bedrock:guidebook`, or `/scriptevent better_on_bedrock:guidebook` if commands are available.

## Main progression

### Reaching the End

The vanilla Eye of Ender route is changed. Craft Eyes of Ender with **five boss eyes** plus an Ender Pearl.

| Eye | Source | Where to look | Rarity / notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Willager Eye | Willager. | Willager Arena in Overworld plains, forests, or birch forests. | Rare; roughly one placement candidate per 1,216×1,216 blocks before biome filtering. |
| Flender Eye | Flender. | Flender Tower in Overworld plains, forests, or birch forests. | Rare; roughly one placement candidate per 800×800 blocks before biome filtering. |
| Enchantaegis Eye | Enchantaegis. | Underground Enchantaegis dungeon in Overworld plains, forests, or birch forests. | Very rare; roughly one placement candidate per 1,984×1,984 blocks before biome filtering. |
| Samurai Eye | Withered Samurai. | Nether Blackstone Castle; the structure summons the boss. | Guaranteed boss-eye drop. |
| Shielded Pyroclast Eye | Shielded Pyroclast/Inferior. | Nether Inferior boss arena. | Rare Nether arena; about 4.55% per placement attempt at y 32-80. |

## Magic, runes, and special items

### Runes and staffs

Runes are traded with the Lone Wizard for staffs. Staffs use durability as mana: hold use for the main attack, or crouch + use for the secondary attack.

| Item | From | Used for | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Basic Rune | Enchantaegis drop and structure loot. | Trade 3 for Basic Staff; also part of specialist staff trades. | Staff currency. |
| Ice Rune | Iceologer drop and structure loot. | Trade 2 Basic Runes + 1 Ice Rune for Ice Staff. | Staff currency. |
| Flame Rune | Nether Bridge and Wizard House loot. | Trade 2 Basic Runes + 1 Flame Rune for Flame Staff. | Staff currency. |
| Flender Rune | Guaranteed Flender drop. | Trade 3 Basic Runes + 1 Flender Rune for Flender Staff. | Staff currency. |

### Nether Amulet

Craft a complete Nether Amulet from Empty Nether Amulet + Withered, Vitality, and Fire Nether Stones. Active forms have 140 durability and can be repaired with Fiery Ingots.

| Control / mode | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Crouch + use | Cycles mode. |
| Inferno Shield mode | Releases an Inferno Shield forward. |
| Wither Skull mode | Fires a dangerous Wither Skull and starts a 3.5-second attack cooldown. |
| Regeneration/fire burst mode | Grants Regeneration III for 30 seconds, deals 15 fire damage to non-player entities within 16 blocks, and spawns a heal particle. |

### Ghost Necklace

| Step | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Get fragments | Flender drops 2 Ghost Necklace Fragments per kill. |
| Craft | 4 fragments make a Fixed Ghost Necklace. |
| Repair | Broken Ghost Necklace + 4 Gold Ingots makes a fixed one. |
| Use | Put a Soul Star in the off-hand, then use the necklace. |
| Effect | Consumes 1 Soul Star and gives 10 seconds of spectator/ghost mode. |
